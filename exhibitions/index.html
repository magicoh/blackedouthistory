Circle of Hate

Hatred thrives in dark places.

Histories of hatred, racism, dehumanization, control, and all other machinations of evil are not forgotten, they are extinguished. They are stamped out, doused with fire hoses, and buried deeply in the ground, not only to be hidden away, but to be stripped of their power... drained of their energy and influence.

For a limited time, select items from our Ku Klux Klan Collection are on loan to the Roots101 African American History Museum in Louisville, KY, so these memories - painful though they are - be exhumed, polished, and displayed to the world to regain their power in the midst of another civil rights crisis in this country.
