Illuminating the Darkest Corners of American History

From Slavery to segregation...From the Jim Crow South to modern political & economic disenfranchisement...the history of People of Color in the US has been redacted, ignored, rewritten, and white-washed.

Blacked Out History's Mission is to shine a light on these histories through the collection and display of relics of a racist past that yet permeates to this day.

Our Work

Collections

We visit flea markets, estate sales, & work with collectors across the US to curate a collection of relics and artifacts that shine a light on historical racism in the US.

Exhibitions

We partner with museums & higher-educational institutions to share our collections with the public (in-person and virtually); light is the best disinfectant.

Outreach

We are building a social & traditional media strategy & platform to share our collections & work with a broader, global audience.

Education

In the past, we have worked with institutions of higher-education to leverage our collections as a supplement for their coursework.
